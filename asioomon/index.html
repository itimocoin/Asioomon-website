WELCOME ADDRESS

A message from the President

On behalf of the National Executive Council, I welcome you to ASIOOMON — the Association of Independent Online and Offline Multi Level Marketers of Nigeria. We exist to dignify the marketing profession, protect our members, and create opportunities that change lives.

Whether you market from a shop in your local community or from a smartphone serving customers across continents, you belong here. Together we are building the most respected community of independent marketers on the African continent.

Amb. Oluwarotimi Oluwatobi AyodeleInterim NEC President · ASIOOMON
